Q6. (20 Marks | 300 words) — CASE STUDY
Divya Sharma, a newly posted Sub-Divisional Magistrate in Rudrapur district, Uttar Pradesh, is known for her impeccable integrity and commitment to constitutional values. The district has recently witnessed escalating communal tensions after a minor dispute between two communities flared up, leading to several incidents of public disorder and apprehension.

As the situation remains volatile, Divya receives direct instructions from a powerful state minister and her immediate superior to take swift and stringent action primarily against members of one specific community, citing 'political necessity' and 'maintaining peace at any cost'. This directive, if implemented, would involve arbitrary arrests and demolition drives targeting one side, clearly violating principles of natural justice and impartiality.

Divya is acutely aware that acceding to this demand would severely undermine the rule of law, erode public trust in the administration, and potentially exacerbate the communal divide. Conversely, defying the political directive could jeopardize her career and invite strong repercussions from powerful political figures.

(a) Identify and discuss the ethical issues involved in this case. (50 words)
(b) What are the options available to Divya Sharma? Evaluate each option with its merits and demerits. (100 words)
(c) What course of action would you recommend and why? How would you handle the pressures involved? (150 words)
